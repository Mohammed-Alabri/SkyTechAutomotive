Our Services

Maintenance

We perform all types of maintenance on Toyota products from basic oil changes to transmission services including sealed transmissions, CVT and eCVT. We also maintain differentials, suspension, brakes, engines and more.

Hybrid

We service all Toyota and Lexus Hybrid vehicles from basic maintenance to High voltage battery replacement, Inverter repair, Hybrid transmission service, Hybrid system diagnosis and repair and more.

Performance

We service all high performance Toyota and Lexus models. Including but not limited to Lexus LFA, Lexus ISF, Lexus GSF, Lexus RCF, Toyota Supra, GR Supra, Toyota Celica, Toyota MR2, Toyota 86, Scion FR-S, Lexus LC and more...

Trucks

We service all Toyota and Lexus Trucks. Stop by for all your Tacoma, Tundra, T100, Pickup, 4Runner, Lexus LX and Lexus GX needs.

Diagnostics

Our state of the art facility is fully equipment with the latest diagnostic equipment to diagnose and repair the most complicated systems in modern Toyota and Lexus models. We have access to the dealership level scan tools and information system. Additionally we service Toyota Safety Sense sensors and cameras including Front radar sensor calibration, Front recognition camera calibration, ISC parking sensor calibration, 360 view camera calibrations and more.

Electric cars

Our modern facility is fully equipped to service and repair the upcoming Toyota BZ4x and Lexus RZ450e electric cars.
